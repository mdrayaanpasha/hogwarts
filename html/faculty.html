Professor Albus Dumbledore

Educated at Hogwarts and other institutions, specializing in Transfiguration. Renowned for his wisdom, mastery of magic, and leadership as the Headmaster of Hogwarts.

Professor Minerva McGonagall

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Transfiguration. A skilled and fair educator, she serves as Deputy Headmistress and Head of Gryffindor House.

Professor Severus Snape

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Potions and later Defense Against the Dark Arts. Known for his exceptional skill in potion-making and expertise in the Dark Arts.

Professor Sybill Trelawney

Specializing in Divination. Known for her eccentric predictions and occasional accurate prophecies, despite her often vague and dreamy demeanor.

Professor Rubeus Hagrid

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Care of Magical Creatures. Known for his deep knowledge and affection for magical creatures, he serves as the Keeper of Keys and Grounds.

Professor Pomona Sprout

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Herbology. Expertise in magical plants and their care, she is the Head of Hufflepuff House and teaches Herbology.

Professor Nicolas Flamel

A renowned alchemist, known for creating the Philosopher's Stone. Although not a Hogwarts professor in a traditional sense, his contributions to alchemy are highly respected in magical circles.

Professor Remus Lupin

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Defense Against the Dark Arts. Noted for his proficiency in defensive spells and his compassion for students, despite his condition as a werewolf.

Professor Dolores Umbridge

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Defense Against the Dark Arts briefly. Known for her strict discipline and controversial methods during her tenure as Hogwarts High Inquisitor.

Professor Filius Flitwick

Educated at Hogwarts, specializing in Charms. Known for his cheerful demeanor and expertise in charm-casting, he is also the Head of Ravenclaw House.

Professor Cuthbert Binns

Specializing in History of Magic. A ghost who lectures in a monotonous voice about historical events to students, largely unaware of his spectral state.
