The Arcane Departments of Hogwarts

Potions Mastery

Explore the art of brewing magical concoctions and mastering the science behind them.

Led by Professor Severus Snape

Divination

Study the art of predicting the future through various mystical methods and interpretations.

Led by Professor Sybill Trelawney

Care of Magical Creatures

Learn to care for and understand magical beasts, their habitats, and behaviors.

Led by Professor Rubeus Hagrid

Herbology

Study magical plants, their properties, and uses in potions, medicine, and everyday magic.

Led by Professor Pomona Sprout

Transfiguration

Master the art of transforming objects and beings into different forms through magic.

Led by Professor Minerva McGonagall

Alchemy

Study the mystical science of alchemy, transforming substances and seeking the Philosopher's Stone.

Led by Professor Nicolas Flamel

Defense Against the Dark Arts

Learn defensive spells, charms, and counter-curses against dark creatures and dark magic.

Led by various professors, including Professor Remus Lupin and Professor Dolores Umbridge

History of Magic

Explore the magical history of wizards and witches, famous magical battles, and ancient artifacts.

Led by Professor Cuthbert Binns

Flying

Master the skill of flying on broomsticks and controlling magical flying creatures.

Led by Madam Rolanda Hooch

Charms

Learn a variety of spells and charms to enhance objects and manipulate the environment.

Led by Professor Filius Flitwick
