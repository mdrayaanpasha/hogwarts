Sports & Pastimes at Hogwarts

Quidditch

The wizarding world's most popular sport, played on broomsticks. Houses compete fiercely, aiming to score points with the Quaffle.

Wizard Chess

A magical chess variant where enchanted pieces battle. Students enjoy this strategic game, commanding pieces with magical prowess.

Gobstones

A magical marble game with a twist: losing stones squirt foul-smelling liquid at the player, adding a humorous edge.

Exploding Snap

A popular card game where cards spontaneously explode, adding excitement and danger to the gameplay.

Dueling Club

Test your spellcasting in a competitive setting. Hone magical abilities and strategic thinking under controlled conditions.
