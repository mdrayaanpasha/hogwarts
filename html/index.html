Hogwarts was founded in the Highlands of Scotland around the 9th or 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff, and Salazar Slytherin. The school was established to educate young wizards and witches and protect them from Muggle persecution. According to legend, Rowena Ravenclaw named Hogwarts after dreaming of a warty hog leading her to a cliff by a lake. Since its inception, Hogwarts has educated most wizarding children in Great Britain and Ireland, keeping its location hidden from Muggles and other wizard ing schools.
