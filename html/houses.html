Gryffindor

Founded by Godric Gryffindor, Gryffindor values courage, bravery, nerve, and chivalry. Its emblematic animal is the lion, and its colors are red and gold. The Head of Gryffindor House is Professor Minerva McGonagall. Students from Gryffindor are known for their daring exploits and willingness to stand up for what is right.

Slytherin

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, Slytherin values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal is the serpent, and its colors are green and silver. The Head of Slytherin House is Professor Severus Snape (later Horace Slughorn and then Severus Snape again). Slytherin students are known for their determination to achieve their goals and their strong sense of fraternity.

Hufflepuff

Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, Hufflepuff values hard work, patience, justice, and loyalty. Its emblematic animal is the badger, and its colors are yellow and black. The Head of Hufflepuff House is Professor Pomona Sprout. Hufflepuff students are known for their inclusivity and dedication to fair play.

Ravenclaw

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, Ravenclaw values intelligence, creativity, learning, and wit. Its emblematic animal is the eagle, and its colors are blue and silver (or blue and bronze in the books). The Head of Ravenclaw House is Professor Filius Flitwick. Ravenclaw students are known for their pursuit of knowledge and love of learning.
